## Assets Checklist - Detailed Specs

### Required Images

**1. Thumbnail (Required)**
- Size: 240x240px
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Must be: Clear, recognizable logo or icon
- No text except your brand name

**2. Gallery Images (3-8 recommended)**
- Size: 1270x760px or 1280x800px
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Show: Key features, UI, use cases
- Add text overlays explaining features
- Keep it visual and scannable

**3. Product Video/GIF (Highly Recommended)**
- Length: 30-60 seconds
- Format: MP4, YouTube, or GIF
- Show: Quick product walkthrough
- Focus: Key value proposition and main features

### Copy Specifications

**1. Tagline**
- Max: 60 characters
- Make it: Clear, compelling, specific
- Example: "Turn your ideas into viral Product Hunt launches"

**2. Description**
- Max: 500 characters
- Include: What it does, who it's for, key benefit
- Example: "LaunchKit helps founders prepare perfect Product Hunt launches with AI-powered checklists, asset templates, and launch day automation. Get 3x more upvotes."

**3. Topics/Tags**
- Choose: 3 relevant tags
- Examples: Productivity, Developer Tools, Marketing, AI, SaaS

## Hunter vs Self-Launch

### Getting a Hunter (Optional but Helpful)
**Pros:**
- Hunters have established audiences
- More initial visibility
- Credibility boost

**How to Find a Hunter:**
- Look for active hunters in your category
- Reach out 1-2 weeks before launch
- Explain your product and why it's special
- Make it easy for them (provide all assets)

### Self-Launching
**Pros:**
- Full control of narrative
- Direct engagement with community
- Authentic maker story

**When to Self-Launch:**
- You have an engaged audience
- You want maximum authenticity
- You can't find the right hunter
